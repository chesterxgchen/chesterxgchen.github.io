Exhibitions

In 2012, I was invited by the Tsinghua
University to have a solo exhibition at
the Tsinghua University Art Museum.
The exhibition, titled “Yueying Zhong’s
Contemporary Ink Painting”, showcased
33 large paintings. I also gave a lecture to
the school’s faculty and students.

In 2012, I was invited by the Luxun
Academy of Fine Arts to showcase my
second solo exhibition at the school’s
art museum. The exhibition featured 55 large
pieces of work. Meanwhile the school held a
conference to discussion my works and
contemporary ink painting.

In 2011, I was invited by the Shenyang
University to have a solo exhibition
titled, “All in Quietness and Stillness”. The
exhibition, held at the school’s art museum,
featured 65 large paintings. In addition,
I also gave several lectures for the school.

In 2009, I was invited by the Fuontbound
University to have a solo exhibition at the
department of fine art. The exhibition, titled,
“Compelling,” featured 35 pieces of works.
During the exhibition I also gave a lecture
for the fine art department.

In 2007, I was invited by the Luxun Academy
of Fine Arts to have solo Exhibition at the school’s
art museum. The exhibition featured 70 large paintings
with ink and color.

In 2007, I had a solo exhibition at the
Zhongbai Art Museum in the Shandong
Province, China..

In 2003, I was invited by the Saint Louis University
to have a solo exhibition at the University Museum of
Art. The show lasted for two and a half months,
featuring 38 pieces of contemporary ink paintings
characterized by Eastern aesthetics and styles.
This exhibit was broadcasted by various media sources.
